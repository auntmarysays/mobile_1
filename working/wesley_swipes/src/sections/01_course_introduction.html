Course Introduction

Upon successful completion of this course, you should be able to do the following:

  • Define unlawful workplace harassment.
  • Describe the two main types of unlawful workplace harassment.
  • Describe the characteristics of a hostile work environment.
  • Define employment discrimination as it relates to the ‘Protected Classes’ under federal anti-discrimination laws.
  • Recognize that inappropriate, disrespectful behavior and bullying are just as unacceptable as unlawful harassment.
  • Recognize the four archetypes of workplace bullying.
  • Demonstrate how to RECOGNIZE, RESPOND TO, and REPORT harassment and discrimination.
  • Understand Scitent’s commitment to providing a safe and secure working environment.
  • Know how to find Scitent’s specific policies and procedures for reporting and investigating a claim.
