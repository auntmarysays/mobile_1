Release date: 11/15/2017

Expiration date: 11/15/2019

Estimated time to complete this activity: 52 minutes.

Target Audience

This activity is intended for healthcare professionals (physicians, physician assistants, respiratory therapists, nurses, perfusionists) engaged in the care of patients with respiratory failure, cardiac failure or combined respiratory and cardiac failure.

Educational Objectives

After completing this activity, the participant should be better able to:

  1. Review of recent literature.
  2. Discuss outcomes and trends.
  3. Review cannulation strategies.
  4. Discuss ethical considerations.
